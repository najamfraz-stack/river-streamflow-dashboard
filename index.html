Why monitor streamflow in Fort Collins?

The Cache la Poudre River is central to Fort Collins' water supply, recreation, and ecological integrity. Understanding how the river's flow changes seasonally and during storms helps planners, engineers, and residents make informed decisions.

Community Safety

Tracking rapid rises in water level improves early warning for flash floods and supports safe management of river-adjacent infrastructure.

Water Management

Streamflow data informs reservoir operations, diversions, and conservation strategies, especially during drought conditions.

Environmental Stewardship

Consistent monitoring supports habitat protection, restoration projects, and long-term climate resilience planning.
